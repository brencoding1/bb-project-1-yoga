Class Styles

Original Hot

Original Hot focuses on slower and less transitioning poses and remains the same consistent poses each class so that you can focus on working in, rather than breaking the meditation with constant flow or switching poses.

Qi Gone

An ancient holistic health practice that combines breath work with flowing movements and mindful intention for balancing the mind, emotions, and body.

Hot Buti

Buti is a movement methodology that incorporates power vinyasa flow with primal movement, cardio-dance and conditioning. The spiral structure technique strengthens deep core muscles, and intentional shaking and vibration releases trauma stored at the cellular level.

Warm Yin

Namaste Yin Yoga is a slow-paced practice targeting connective tissue, especially in the hips. Suitable for all levels, it is great complement to other more Yang exercises. Set to approximately 80 degrees.
